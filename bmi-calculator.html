BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a quick screening tool that helps you estimate if your weight is healthy for your height. It’s a widely used metric calculated from your weight and height, and is used to categorize you as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

WHO BMI Classification

Category BMI Range
Underweight< 18.5
Normal weight18.5 – 24.9
Overweight25.0 – 29.9
Obese30.0 and above
